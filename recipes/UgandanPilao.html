Uganda Pilao

Ingredients

  • rice
  • meat -beef
  • cooking oil
  • salt
  • onions
  • water
  • tomatoes
  • pilau masala
    •

    STEPS TO FOLLOW â€“ our local METHOD

    1.Chop the beef into desired pieces.

    2.place a clean saucepan on an already lit charcoal stove and then put cooking oil into the saucepan.

    3.let the cooking oil get ready and place your chopped beef in the saucepan with cooking oil.

    4.stir the beef, when it turns golden brown add salt to your beef after adding salt stir again such that salt is evenly distributed in the beef.

    5.move-extend the beef to one side of the saucepan and put onions to the remaining part of the saucepan

    6.After some two minutes stir well to mix the onions with the beef fry until the onions turn brown

    7.Add 1/2 cup of water to the mixture to avoid it from burning and again move the mixture to one side of the saucepan and tomatoes to the remaining side

    8.After adding tomatoes stir well and mix the tomatoes with the mixture , keep stirring little by little until tomatoes turn brown

    9.Add pilau masala to the mixture and stir well add 1/2 cup of water to mixture

    10.Add 1kg of basmati rice and stir well

    11.Add 4 1/2 cups of water to the mixture and evenly stir ,leave it to boil for some 2minutes and stir well

    12.

    14.check after some minutes when the food is ready remove it from the charcoal stove and serve.
